Simple Slip

A voice-first micro-app that replaces the paper pad, price notebook, and mystery stock transfers for a tier-3 kirana shop.

Goal: Digitise slips, prices and internal transfers with zero accounting jargon and almost no typing. If parents can use WhatsApp voice notes, they can use this.

3. User Flows (tap-count shown)

3.1 Quick Slip (≤ 4 taps)

  1. Tap mic on home screen.
  1. Speak: “दस किलो चीनी ₹45” → Google voice-to-text fills fields.
  1. Tap ‘✓’ to confirm totals auto-calculated.
  1. Tap ‘Print’ or ‘WhatsApp’. Done.

3.2 Price Board (admin: 5 taps, staff: 1 tap)

  • Owner: open Prices, edit cell, tap ‘Publish’.
  • Staff: open Prices (auto-refreshed), read. No edit rights.

3.3 Stock Move (≤ 5 taps)

  1. Swipe to Transfer.
  1. Choose item (search list).
  1. Enter qty (numpad).
  1. Source = Wholesale (default), Dest = Retail.
  1. Tap ‘Send’. Both inventories adjust.

3.4 Day Summary (0 taps)

  • At 21:00 an automated workflow exports today’s slips + transfers to PDF and WhatsApps it to the partners’ group.

6. Risks & Mitigations

RiskMitigation
Voice-to-text mis-hears prices in noisy shop.Numeric field can be tapped to over-type; totals auto-recalc.
Staff forget to refresh price board.App auto-refreshes every open; plus 9 am WhatsApp push of the sheet.
Connectivity drops.Glide caches locally; syncs when back online. Slips can still be printed.
